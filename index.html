ABOUT DJ LAKHAN

DJ Lakhan

A renowned Indian DJ, music producer, lyricist, and influencer from Nandurbar, Maharashtra, India. Known for creating electrifying remixes and original compositions, DJ Lakhan has carved a unique space in the Indian music industry. Since launching his YouTube channel in May 2021, he has amassed over 138K subscribers and 43+ million views with 428 videos.
